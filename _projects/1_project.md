A Python toolkit for **Pauli Propagation–based** quantum algorithm implementation, developed on the
Quantum AI Team at Norma. It enables efficient simulation of operator (Heisenberg-picture) evolution and
the implementation of diverse quantum algorithms — including **QAOA** and **VQE** — with reduced quantum
resource requirements.

Key directions explored with the toolkit:

- Efficient classical simulation of variational quantum circuits via Pauli propagation.
- Noise-resilience analysis of VQCs (VQE, QAOA) under both simulators and real hardware.
- Train-on-classical / deploy-on-quantum workflows for NISQ-era algorithms.
