I am **Shawn Hyunwoo Kim (김현우)**, a quantum algorithm researcher on the **Quantum AI Team at [Norma](https://norma.co.kr/)**, where I develop **quantum machine learning (QML)** algorithms. My current work centers on **Pauli propagation** (I co-developed Norma's GPU-accelerated *Pado-Pauli* toolkit), **quantum generative models** for drug discovery, and **noise-resilient variational quantum circuits**.

I studied **Physics** and **Artificial Intelligence** (double major) at **Sogang University**, with earlier hands-on research in experimental quantum optics (POSTECH) and 2D quantum materials. My broader interest is in bridging machine learning and quantum computing — designing algorithms that are both theoretically grounded and practically runnable on near-term quantum hardware.

I am currently preparing for graduate study in quantum computing / quantum machine learning. You can find my work on [GitHub](https://github.com/Shawn-Hyunwoo) and [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=AtdnRUEAAAAJ&hl=en), and reach me by email at kimhw7537@gmail.com.
